Heading: Islamabad United win toss bowl against Lahore Qalandar

Article: DUBAI: Islamabad United captain Misbah-ul-Haq won the toss and elected to bowl first against Lahore Qalandars in the 20th Twenty20 match of the Pakistan Super League 2016 here at the Duabi International Cricket Stadium Wednesday night.The last game of the league stage, and a critical game for Lahore Qalandars. They have to win this to qualify for the play-offs. If they don't, Karachi Kings will qualify in their expense. Islamabad United are already through.For Lahore, Chris Gayle is out with a back injury. Naved Yasin is also out while there are a lot of changes in Islamabad team.Teams:Islamabad United: Sharjeel Khan, Dwayne Smith, Khalid Latif, Asif Ali, Misbah-ul-Haq, Brad Haddin, Azhar Mahmood, Saeed Ajmal, Ashar Zaidi, Rumman Raees, Mohammad Irfan.Lahore Qalandars: Azhar Ali, Cameron Delport, Sohaib Maqsood, Dwayne Bravo, Mohammad Rizwan, Umar Akmal, Kevon Cooper, Ajantha Mendis, Ehsan Adil, Adnan Rasool, Zia-ul-Haq.
