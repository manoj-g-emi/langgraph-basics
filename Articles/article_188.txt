Heading: cng stations to remain shut on 2nd consecutive day tuesday

Article: KARACHI: CNG stations will remain shut in Sindh on second consecutive day, Tuesday and will reopen on Wednesday morning after 48 hours of closure.According to Sui Southern Gas Company, closure of CNG outlets has been extended due to shortage of gas supply.Now, CNG pumps will reopen at 8:00AM on Wednesday morning after 48 hours of closure.It may be noted here that SSGC often changes CNG closure schedule, resulting daily commuter suffer severely.
