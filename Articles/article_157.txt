Heading: nepra approves rs1.75 per unit cut in k electric tariff

Article: KARACHI: The National Electric Power Regulatory Authority (NEPRA) has approved Rs1.75 per unit cut in the tariff of K-Electric to provide a relief the Karachiites with effect from next month.The consumers will benefit from the cut in the tariff from the June.

Date: 5/22/2015
