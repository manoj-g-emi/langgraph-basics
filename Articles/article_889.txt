Heading: Sale of CNG in litres illegal in Sindh OGRA

Article: strong>KARACHI: Oil and Gas Regulation Authority (OGRA) spokesman has categorically stated that Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) cannot be sold in litres in Sindh province.</strongThe spokesman for the regulatory body said, Sale of CNG in litre in Sindh is illegal. Strict actions will be taken against those violating the orders.He said OGRA has sent letter to CNG Associations regarding the issue. Different inspection teams have been formed to collect data of CNG pumping stations in Karachi.
