Heading: pol prices likely to see major hike from nov 1

Article: ISLAMABAD: The government is expected to ratchet up the prices of all petroleum products by a large margin with effect from November 1, sources told Geo News on Wednesday.It is expected that the price of petrol will be increased by Rs 5.35 per litre, High Speed Diesel by Rs 2.43, High Octane Blending Component (HOBC) by Rs 6.10, and kerosene oil by Rs 3.17 per litre.According to sources, Oil and Gas Regulatory Authority (OGRA) is in the process of finalising a summary which will be forwarded tomorrow.
