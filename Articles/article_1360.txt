Heading: Rain stops in Dhaka toss likely in few minu

Article: DHAKA: The rain stopped at the Shere Bangla National Stadium in Mirpur and the toss is likely to take place in a while later in the final match of the Asia Cup T20I between Bangladesh and India on Sunday night.Both the teams are getting warm-up for the biggest event of the tournament.The match was delayed due to heavy rain with storm here in Mirpur as treams of water were flowing off the covers the frame around the big screen at the venue was partially blown away by the wind.However, ground staff worked hard to clear the water from the ground and now the covers have been removed from the pitch and the stumps have been put in.This is Bangladesh's first appearance in any T20I tournament that involves three or more teams.Meanwhile, India are playing their third final in such events.
