Heading: Uncapped Bumrah to replace Shami in India T20 squad

Article: MUMBAI: Fast bowler Jasprit Bumrah will replace injured Mohammed Shami in India´s squad for the three Twenty20 internationals against Australia, the country´s cricket board said on Monday.The 25-year-old has not played international cricket since finishing as India´s second-highest wicket taker at last year´s World Cup with 17 victims.Bumrah underwent surgery in March and was named in the squad for the limited-overs series in Australia, only to be ruled out again with a hamstring injury.The 22-year-old Bumrah sprung to prominence playing for the Mumbai Indians franchise in the Indian Premier League and is the joint-highest wicket-taker in his country´s domestic Twenty20 competition this season. (Reuters)
