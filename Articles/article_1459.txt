Heading: Imran to advice Pak team ahead of India T20 

Article: strong>ISLAMABAD: Chairman Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) Imran Khan on Friday departed for India to watch Pak-India World Cup T20 match on March 19 in Kolkata.</strongSpeaking to media at Bani Gala before his departure for India, Imran Khan who was captain of Pakistan team that won 1992 World cup said, I will meet the players ahead of the match and will try to advise them in light of my experience.Khan said Pak-India T20 match is very important, adding that he could mentally prepare Pakistani team.PTI chief T20 skipper Shahid Khan Afridi had sought guidance from him.He went on to say that Sheikh Rasheed was to fly with him but had left earlier.
