Heading: pia offers 25pc discount on domestic tickets for eidul az

Article: KARACHI: On the occasion of Eidul Azha, PIA is offering 25 per cent ticket discount on all domestic sectors for travel during September 25, 26 and 27. A statement issued here on Tuesday said that the discount offer was formally announced by PIAs Director Marketing & Customer Services Khurram Mushtaq at the airlines head office today.The passengers can now avail 25 per cent discount on all domestic flights during the first three days of Eid. The discount offer applies to all three classes; Business, Economy and Economy Plus.
