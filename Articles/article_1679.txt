Heading: KPK bat against Punjab in Pakistan Cup fi

Article: FAISALABAD: Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KPK) won the toss and elected to bat against Punjab in the day-night final of the Pakistan One-day Cup here on Sunday.Younis Khan is back after the umpiring incident and is leading the KPK team, who made two changes replacing Adil Amin and Israrullah for Younis and Musadiq Ahmed. On the other hand Punjab remain unchanged.Teams:KPK: Ahmed Shehzad, Fakhar Zaman, Zohaib Khan, Bismillah Khan (wk), Musadiq Ahmed, Younis Khan (captain), Fahim Ashraf, Yasir Shah, Sameen Gul, Zia-ul-Haq, Mohammad Asghar.Punjab: Shan Masood, Salman Butt, Shoaib Malik (captain), Asad Shafiq, Mohammad Rizwan (wk), Amad Butt, Saif Badar, Aamer Yamin, Kashif Bhatti, Zulfiqar Babar, Ehsan Adil.
