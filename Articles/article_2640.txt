Heading: Prices of petrol diesel up by one ru

Article: ISLAMABAD: The Government has announced to raise prices of petroleum products by one rupee here on Wednesday.Finance minister Ishaq Dar while talking with media regarding changes in prices of petroleum products told that an increase of one rupee each in prices of petrol and diesel had been made while the Government would give a subsidy on kerosene oil and light diesel. Thus, the previous prices of light diesel and kerosene oil will remain unchanged.He said the new prices of the petrol products would effective from 12 tonight.
