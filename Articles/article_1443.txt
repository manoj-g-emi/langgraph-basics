Heading: Sri Lanka beat Afghanistan by six wickets in World T20

Article: strong>KOLKATA : Champions Sri Lanka beat qualifiers Afghanistan by six wickets in their first Super 10 Group One match of the World Twenty20 in Kolkata on Thursday.</strongOpting to bat first, Afghanistan overcame a slow start to post 153-7 and Sri Lanka romped home in 18.5 overs with Tillakaratne Dilshan top scoring with an unbeaten 83.
