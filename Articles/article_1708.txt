Heading: Pakistan beat India to retain Asia Kabaddi Cu

Article: WAH CANTT: Pakistan defended their title in Asia Kabaddi Cup 2016 defeating India by 19 points here on Friday.They achieved 50 points against 31 by their arch rivals in the final of the tournament.Pakistan won the title for the second time in a row.Earlier, both Pakistan and India remained unbeaten in their pool matches of the tournament.Pakistan had won their pool matches against Iran, Afghanistan, Nepal and Sri Lanka.
