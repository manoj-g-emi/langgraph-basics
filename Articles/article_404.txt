Heading: Half a million tax payers file e Returns this year

Article: ISLAMABAD: The number of e-Return filers in Pakistan has climbed to 500,000 compared to only 200,000 filers the previous year, sources at Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) said.The sources further said that the tax payers are being provided the facility of filing their returns online.The number of tax returns filers is witnessing a steady rise, the sources said.
