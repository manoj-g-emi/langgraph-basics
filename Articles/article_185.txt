Heading: pia slashes fares for domestic travel during ramaz

Article: KARACHI: Pakistan International Airlines (PIA) has slashed fares for domestic travel during the holy month of Ramazan. The national flag carrier will reduce fares by 20 per cent during the hold month for passengers travelling on economy, economy plus and business classes. Passengers can purchase tickets at the reduced fares from 19 June till 14 July.
