Heading: Bank holiday to be observed on July 1

Article: KARACHI: The State Bank of Pakistan will remain closed for public dealings on July 1, 2016 (Friday) which will be observed as Bank Holiday.All banks, Development Finance Institutions (DFIs); Micro Finance Banks (MFBs) shall, therefore, remain closed for public dealings on the aforesaid date.However, all employees of the banks / DFIs / MFBs will attend the office as usual.
