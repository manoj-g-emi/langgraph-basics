Heading: Uruguay wants to co host 2030 Football World Cu

Article: COLONIA: Argentina and neighboring Uruguay will bid to co-host the 2030 World Cup, Argentine President Mauricio Macri said at a joint news conference on Thursday with Uruguayan leader Tabare Vazquez. "We have decided that our best opportunity is to jointly nominate ourselves as candidates," Macri said during a visit to Colonia, Uruguay. Uruguay hosted and won the first World Cup in 1930, beating Argentina 4-2 in the final.
