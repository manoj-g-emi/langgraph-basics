Article: strong>ISLAMABAD: The Pakistan International Airline (PIA) on Wednesday announced reduction in domestic and international fares.</strongPIA spokesman Danyal Gilani said that PIA has reduced Umrah fares by Rs4000. The PIA has also reduced Islamabad-Karachi fares to Rs7500 from Rs9,000.He went on to say that 10 percent reduction has also been made on international flights including London, Manchester, New York, Pairs, Oslo and Toronto routes.
