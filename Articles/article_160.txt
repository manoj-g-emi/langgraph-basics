Article: ISLAMABAD: The Federal Cabinet on Tuesday approved the budget strategy paper, sources revealed to Geo News. During the cabinet meeting, Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif said tax rate had to be reduced to increase revenue. He added that people would happily pay taxes if the rate was reduced. The prime minister directed the cabinet to provide maximum relief to people in the budget, emphasising that the economic impact should reach people.
