Heading: Islamabad set 153 for Peshawar in PSL T20

Article: SHARJAH: Islamabad set a modest target of 153 runs for Peshawar Zalmi in the 13th Twenty20 match of the Pakistan Super League here at the Sharjah Cricket Stadium on Friday night.After being put in by Peshawar Zalmi, Islamabad United made 152 for six in 20 overs.Khalid latif top-scored with 59 runs in 41 balls. He hit four sixes and a four.Other main scorers were captain Misbah-ul-Haq (32 off 27 balls with two sixes and three fours), Sharjeel Khan (28 off 22 balls with two sixes and two fours) and Shane Watson (19 off 20 balls with four fours).For Peshawar Zalmi, Wahab Riaz and Mohammad Asghar claimed two wickets each.
