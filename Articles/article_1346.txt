Heading: Pakistan win toss bowl against Sri L

Article: DHAKA: Pakistan captain Shahid Khan Afridi won the toss and opted for fielding first in the dead rubber against Sri Lanka in their last Twent20 International of the Asia Cup 2016 here at the Shere Bangla National Stadium, Mirpur on Friday.Pakistan have made three changes in the team, bringing in debutant Iftikhar Ahmed, left-arm spinner Mohammad Nawaz and left-arm quick Wahab Riaz in place of Khurram Manzoor, Mohammad Sami and Anwar Ali.Sri Lanka are being led by Dinesh Chandimal, their third captain in the tournament, after Angelo Mathews' calf strain forced him to sit out. Mathews was replaced by wicketkeeper-batsman Niroshan Dickwella.Teams:Pakistan: Mohammad Hafeez, Sharjeel Khan, Sarfraz Ahmed, Umar Akmal, Shoaib Malik, Shahid Afridi (captain), Iftikhar Ahmed, Wahab Riaz, Mohammad Nawaz, Mohammad Amir, Mohammad IrfanSri Lanka: Dinesh Chandimal, Tillakaratne Dilshan, Shehan Jayasuriya, Chamara Kapugedara, Niroshan Dickwella, Milinda Siriwardana, Dasun Shanaka, Thisara Perera, Nuwan Kulasekara, Rangana Herath, Dushmantha Chameera.
