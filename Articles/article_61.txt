Heading: price of sugar decreases in karachi

Article: KARACHI: The price of sugar decreased by 50 paisas in the wholesale market and is now being sold at Rs 49 per kilo. According to sources in the wholesale market, the price decrease took place due to an increase in the supply of sugar. The price decrease is also being experienced at the retail level where sugar is being sold at Rs 52 which is an Rs 1-2 decrease.
