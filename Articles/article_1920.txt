Article: strong>LAHORE: Newly Head Coach of Pakistan cricket team Mickey Arthur has said that Pakistan need to improve its rankings and have to become more consistent.</strongIn his first media talk as the Pakistan coach he disclosed that he had a real plan to lift Pakistan cricket out of the dumps and hopes the players will get rid all their fears as they start a new chapter.He said he does not care if the team makes mistakes. Professionals make mistakes, he said."I don't want us to fear failure. I think any team that fears failure is a team that struggles, he added."I will manage issues on a day-to-day basis to make sure they don't impact the team's performance," Arthur told the reporter.Mickey Arthur who arrived here on Wednesday night called on Chairman Pakistan Cricket Board Shaharyar M Khan at Gadaffi stadium."I have had fruitful discussions with all stakeholders, he concluded.During the meeting the newly appointed coach gave his professional opinion to lift teams performance besides discussing ways and means to enhance teams performance during the tour of England.He also discussed with the Chairman PCB his plan for the betterment of Pakistan cricket and the Chairman highlighted the priorities of the PCB in this regard said a spokesman of PCB.
