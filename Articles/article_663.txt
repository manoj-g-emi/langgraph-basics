Article: strong>DUBAI: Beauty world Middle East, the largest international trade fair for beauty products, hair, fragrances and well-being in the Middle East, has closed its doors in Dubai at the Dubai International Convention and Exhibition Centre, after three very lively days of business featuring more than 1,500 exhibitors from 60 countries.</strongMore than 30,000 trade visitors made their way to Dubai to see world´s largest spectrum of hair, fragrances, beauty products and wellness. Dr. Saeed Qadir Mangi, Commercial counselor of Pakistan in Dubai also visited the show. The exhibitors appreciated the role of Commercial counselor and the staff in Dubai.21 Pakistani premium exhibitors have showcased their products in the fair Beauty world Dubai. Ten exhibitors participated under the pavilion of Trade Development Authority Government of Pakistan. Trade Development Authority of Pakistan organized a joint pavilion with 10 exhibitors.One of the exhibitors Farman Nawaz said, We had a very good business on the first day of show. Buyers from Saudi, UAE, India, Jordan, and Nigeria visited our stand &amp; we had very serious meetings &amp; dealings with them. It´s overall a good exhibition for us, we launched a new product Henna Cone, which we have very good response on, especially buyers from Iran are very interested in it. said Mr. Sameer another exhibitor.Ghazanfar Jafri, a director sales said that, It´s out of our expectations, we had a very good response from the buyers from UAE, Oman, Saudia &amp; Kuwait.Second half of the first day was very good. said Mr. Haris, a director marketing.
