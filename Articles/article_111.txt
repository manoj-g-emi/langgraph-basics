Heading: dar reviews supply of lng to three power plants in punjab

Article: ISLAMABAD: A high-level meeting in Islamabad on Sunday reviewed ways for smooth and regular supply of LNG to three  power plants in Punjab with a total capacity of 3,600 megawatts.The meeting presided by Finance Minister Ishaq Dar was attended by senior federal and Punjab government officials.These power plants each of 1200-megawatt capacity are to be constructed at Kasur, Sheikhupura and Jhang districts.The meeting was apprised that the federal government will lay an eleven hundred-Kilometer long North-South pipeline to transport the LNG, to the plants.The Finance Minister said a mechanism would also be devised to supply LNG to these plants.
