Heading: home world set to distribute kiwi mobiles in paki

Article: KARACHI: Home World, a Pakistan based company, has signed a contract with ICS New Zealand for distribution of Kiwi Mobiles - a well reputed brand of New Zealand for Asia and United Arab Emirates (UAE).The ceremony was held in Karachi, Pakistan, in which both the parties announced their aim to establish quality and latest technology driven products to cater to the present day market.The agreement was inked by Chairman ICS Mr. Richard Prebble and CEO Home World (Right) Asif Rajar.Soft launch dates will be announced in Pakistan soon.
