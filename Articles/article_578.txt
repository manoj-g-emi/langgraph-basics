Heading: Sargodha Cases for electricity defaulters forwarded to NAB

Article: strong>SARGODHA: WAPDA/Fesco has forwarded cases of 17 defaulters to the National Accountability Bureau (NAB) while criminal cases have been lodged against a majority of the above defaulters.</strongAccording to WAPDA/Fesco sources, cases of more than a dozen defaulters in several districts of Sargodha and Mianwali have been forwarded to NAB.Dues of hundreds of thousands stand outstanding against these defaulters but despite repeated reminders they have failed to deposit these dues in the national exchequer.After the above development, the NAB sent notices to the defaulters to clear their dues.
