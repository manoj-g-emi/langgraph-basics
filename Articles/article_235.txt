Heading: gas supply to punjab cng stations to be restored

Article: LAHORE: The Sui Northeren Gas Pipelines have decided to restore gas supply to CNG sector in Punjab that was closed on July 30.According to a spokesman for the SNGPL, gas supply to all the CNG stations would be restored today (Friday) at 6:00 pm.He said the supply would continue until the next order is issued.
