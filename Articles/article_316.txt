Heading: dar prevents sons undertaking business ventures in paki

Article: ISLAMABAD: Federal Minister for Finance, Ishaq Dar on Tuesday said he has prevented his sons from undertaking any business venture in Pakistan.Speaking in Geo News program Capital Talk hosted by senior anchor Hamid Mir, Dar said as long as he is in the field of politics he would ensure that his sons never become part of any business activity in Pakistan.He said he reserves the right to go into litigation against National Accountability Bureau (NAB) which, according to him, hurled allegations at him over an anonymous complaint.
