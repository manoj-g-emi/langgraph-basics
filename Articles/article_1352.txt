Heading: Pakistani cricketers talk good about India for money Sehwag

Article: strong>Virender Sehwag says former Pakistani cricketers want to make money and for that reason they are praising Indian players.</strongDuring his talk with comedian Vikram Sathye, the former opening batsman targeted Rawalpindi Express Shoaib Akhtar and former fast bowler Rana Navidul Hassan.When asked why Shoaib Akhtar has been praising local players, Sehwag said Pakistani players know  India is a huge market for cricket. If they talk good about Indian cricket, they will get business, He said.He said Rana Navidul Hassan was talking a lot of good things about Indian cricket, hoping tv channels would offer him a job.
