Heading: nepra notifies relief of rs 3.24 per unit for consumer

Article: ISLAMABAD: The National Electric Power Regulatory Authority (NEPRA) issued a notification on Tuesday regarding provision of relief of Rs 3.24 per unit to power consumers.According to the notification, the regulatory authority decided the reduction after a review of the fuel price adjustment for December 2014.The relief would be provided to all consumers except for those belonging to K-Electric.
