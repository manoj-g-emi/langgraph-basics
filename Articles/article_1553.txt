Article: KARACHI: Pakistan Twenty20 cricket team captain, Shahid Afridi has apologized to the nation for not being able to live to the expectations of the people in the T20 World Cup.In a video message, the skipper said he is only answerable to the nation and he does not care for others.When I enter into the field to play, I do it with the national spirit, he said.
