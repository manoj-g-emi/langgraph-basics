Heading: National Women Netball Championship to kick off on March 3

Article: strong>KARACHI: Pakistan Netball Federation (PNF) is set to organize 15th National Women Netball Championship from March 3 at Pakistan Sports Complex in Islamabad.</strongA total of 16 teams namely Army, Wapda, Police, Air Force, Navy, Railway, Higher Education Commission, Punjab, KPK, Balochistan, Sindh, Islamabad, FATA, AJK, Lahore Grammar School, LUMS University and Gilgit Balististan will participate in the tournament.Sindh team will defend the title of the tournament.Concluding ceremony of the Championship will be held on March 7.The championship was initially scheduled for January 2016 but due to South Asian Games preparation for the local event was postponed.
