Heading: T20 World Cup trophy reaches Paki

Article: PESHAWAR:  The trophy of the ICC T20 World Cup has reached Pakistan from South Africa and it will be launched in Peshawar tomorrow.The trophy, which began its world-wide tour from Mumbai has now reached Pakistan after completing its tour of South Africa.The T20 World Cup will be played in India in March this year.  The launching ceremony of the trophy will be held at the Army Public School Peshawar at 8-15 in the morning. Pakistan ODI captain Azhar Ali has already arrived in Peshawar to attend the ceremony.From Peshawar, the trophy will go to Lahore on January 12 before leaving for Bangladesh. The world tour of the trophy will finally end at the Indian capital New Delhi on February1.
