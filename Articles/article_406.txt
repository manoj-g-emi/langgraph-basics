Heading: NEPRA cuts power tariff by Rs 206 per uni

Article: strong>ISLAMABAD: National Electric Power Regulatory Authority ( NEPRA) has approved reduction in power tariff by Rs 2.06 per unit for the month of November.</strongNEPRA took up for hearing application filed by Central Power Purchasing Agency (CPPA) seeking reduction in power tariff for the said.During the course of hearing, NEPRA curtailed the tariff by Rs 2.06 per unit against fuel adjustment surcharge for the month of November keeping in view statistics presented by CPPA.According to NEPRA the reduction in tariff will not be applicable to consumers of K-Electric and domestic consumers using upto 300 units electricity per month.
