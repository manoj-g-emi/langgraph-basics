Abdul Razzak of Bangladesh has grabbed 7 wickets in 7 games against Pakistan.Tamim Iqbal of Bangladesh is the leading run scorer from the qualifying stage of WorldT20 this year, with 233 runs off 147 balls and being dismissed just once in three innings.
