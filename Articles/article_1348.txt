Heading: Poor performance in Asia Cup Fact finding committee formed

Article: LAHORE: Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB) has formed a five-member fact-finding committee to probe the causes of Pakistan cricket teams worst performance in the Asia Cup Twenty20 tournament .The committee, comprised of Misbah-ul-Haq, Younis Khan, Subhan Ahmed, Iqbal Qasim and Shakeel Shaikh, will look into the matters of the national teams defeat against India and Bangladesh in Asia Cup matches played in Dhaka.The committee will submit its report to chairman PCB Shaharyar Khan after the completion of the investigations.The Green-Shirts are facing severe criticism for their pathetic performance in the Asia Cup 2016.
