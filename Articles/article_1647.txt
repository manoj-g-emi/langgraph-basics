Heading: Table Tennis Master Cup to be held in May

Article: MULTAN: The Pakistan Table Tennis Federation will hold Table Tennis Master Cup in Peshawar in the next month.According to sports sources, a schedule of matches would be announced soon while matches will be played on league system.The table tennis players including 32 men and 16 women, who had performed well during the 52nd National Table Tennis championship in Multan, would participate in the cup, the sources added.
