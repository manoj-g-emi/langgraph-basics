Heading: nepra approves rs2.88 per unit cut in power tariff for september

Article: ISLAMABAD: National Electric Power Regulatory Authority (NEPRA) has approved Rs2.88 per unit cut in power tariff for the month of September.The authority approved the reduction while hearing on plea for fuel adjustment for the month of September at NEPRA headquarters.Speaking on the occasion, NEPRA Chairman Tariq Sadozzai said consumers will get a relief of Rs24.39 billion, adding that the decision is applicable for all the power distribution companies except K-Electric.
