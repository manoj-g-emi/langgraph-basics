Heading: Karachi Kings set 153 run target for Peshawar Zalmi

Article: DUBAI: Karachi Kings made 152 runs for seven wickets in 20 overs against Peshawar Zalmi in the 19th T20 match of the Pakistan Super League 2016 here at the Dubai International Cricket Stadium on Wednesday.After Karachi Kings captain Shoaib Malik won the toss and decided to bat first, his team could not make any big score.Lendl Simmons top scored with 49, hitting six fours and two sixes in his 37-ball innings.Other main scorers were Ravi Bopara (23), Shahzaib Hasan (18), Shoaib Malik (15) and Bilawal Bhatti (15 not out).Lendl Simmons and Riki Wessels have been included in Karachi Kings while Jim Allenby is playing for Peshawar Zalmi. Tamim Iqbal is not playing in this match.This would be a must win match for Karachi Kings whereas Peshawar Zalmi has already qualified for play-off.
