Heading: Petrol price likely to go up by Rs3 per litre from May 01

Article: strong>ISLAMABAD: Oil and Gas Regulatory Authority (OGRA) has recommended increase in petroleum product prices from May 01, sources said.</strongAccording to the sources, the regulatory body has finalized its working to fix the fuel prices from next month.The summery will be sent to Ministry for Petroleum and Natural Resources today (Thursday), the sources added.According to the summery, the OGRA has recommended Rs04 per litre increase in petrol price, High Speed Diesel by Rs4.41 and kerosene oil by Rs0.16 per litre.
