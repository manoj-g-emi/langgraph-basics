Heading: India overpower Tigers to become Asian 

Article: DHAKA: India defeated Bangladesh by eight wickets in the rain-affected final of the T20 Asia Cup here at the Shere Bangla National Stadium Mirpur on Sunday night.Chasing a rather daunting target of 121, India won the match with ease in 13.5 overs to become the champions of the Asia Cup, played for the first time on T20 format.After losing Rohit Sharma for one, in the second over, when they were only five, Shikhar Dhawan and Virat Kohli took India out of trouble and added 94 runs from 67 balls before Dhawan departed.He made 60 off 44 balls with nine fours and a six.Then it became easier for Kohli and captain Mahinder Singh Dhoni to score the required 22 runs from only seven balls to reach the target.Kohli scored an unbeaten 41 off 28 balls with five boundaries while Dhoni remained not out on 20, made off just six balls with two towering sixes and one four.
