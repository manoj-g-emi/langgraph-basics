Heading: pol prices expected to rise in november

Article: KARACHI: The prices of petroleum products are expected to increase next month (November) in Pakistan. According to sources the price of petrol will increase by Rs3.43/litre and diesel by Rs.2.80/litre. The price of kerosene oil will rise by Rs3.00/litre. The final estimate for the price increase will be made on October 26.
