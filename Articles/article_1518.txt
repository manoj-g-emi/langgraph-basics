Dasun Shanaka joined him and both took the score to 155 but two wickets fell in the 19th over - Shanaka and then Herath were gone.In the final over, they needed 15 runs to win but Ben Stokes bowled intelligently to concede only four runs and England emerged victorious in the end.Mathews remained not out on 73.
