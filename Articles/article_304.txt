Heading: ogra recommends reducing pol pri

Article: ISLAMABAD: The Oil and Gas Regulatory Authority confirmed on Tuesday that will send a summary to the Petroleum Ministry seeking a decrease in the price of petroleum products for October 2015.According to sources, OGRA has recommended a 10-paisa decrease in the price of petrol, and a Rs. 2.00/liter decrease in the price of diesel, HOBC and Kerosene.
